4. Organic Alternatives:

5. Integrated Pest Management (IPM):
Combine multiple strategies, including pruning, fungicides, and cultural practices, to effectively control early blight.

Resistant varieties can minimize disease severity and spread.

Proper soil health can help potato plants resist disease more effectively.
